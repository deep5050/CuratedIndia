India: Curated

The Ultimate Bucket List

India Gate

A monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi, dedicated to the troops of British India who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919. A prominent landmark reflecting incredible national pride.

💡 Enjoy a leisurely walk along the Rajpath and taste street food nearby.
View on Map

Gateway of India

An iconic arch-monument built in the early 20th century in the city of Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It is a vital symbol of India and the most famous spot in the city.

💡 Visit during the golden hour or at night when it's beautifully lit.
View on Map

Hawa Mahal

The Palace of Winds. A magnificent pink sandstone palace with over 900 intricately carved windows built in 1799.

💡 The best time to take photos is early morning when the sun hits the facade.
View on Map

Mahabalipuram

An ancient town known for its temples and monuments built by the Pallava dynasty.

💡 A UNESCO World Heritage site full of history, stone carvings, and beaches.
