# Gateway of India

![Gateway of India](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1570168007204-dfb528c6958f?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&auto=format&fit=crop&w=1000&q=80)

An iconic arch-monument built in the early 20th century in the city of Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It is a vital symbol of India and the most famous spot in the city.

**Note:** Visit during the golden hour or at night when it's beautifully lit.
