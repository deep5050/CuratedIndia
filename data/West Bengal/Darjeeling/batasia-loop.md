# Batasia Loop

![Batasia Loop](assets/images/batasia-loop.jpg)

A spiral railway created to lower the gradient of ascent of the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

**Note:** It offers a panoramic view of Darjeeling town and snowy Mount Kanchenjunga.

[Google Maps link](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BatasiaLoop)

At the center of the Batasia Loop is the War Memorial for the Gorkha soldiers.
