# Mirik Lake

![Sumendu Lake](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1594589088656-788b1f54cfdf?w=1400&q=80)

Mirik is renowned for its beautiful, serene Sumendu Lake surrounded by a garden of tall dark Japanese cedars. The view of Kanchenjunga reflected in the lake's water is simply mesmerizing. 

**Note:** Enjoy horseback riding and boating across the lake, or take a peaceful stroll across the arched footbridge!
