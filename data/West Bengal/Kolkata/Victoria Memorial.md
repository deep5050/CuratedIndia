# Victoria Memorial

![Victoria](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/ec/Victoria_Memorial_-_Kolkata.jpg)

A large marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, which was built between 1906 and 1921.

**Note:** It is dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria. Beautiful to visit in the evening.
