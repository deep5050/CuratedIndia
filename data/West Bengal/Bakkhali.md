# Bakkhali

![Bakkhali](https://www.indyatour.com/images/india/west-bengal/bakkhali-sea-beach-west-bengal.jpg)

Bakkhali is a coastal town in the South 24 Parganas district of West Bengal, India. It is a popular tourist destination, known for its beautiful beaches and scenic beauty.

**Note:** Don't miss the sunrise and sunset from the beach. Also, visit the nearby Jambu Dwip island.
