# Gir National Park

![Gir Lions](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1614027164847-1b28cfe1df60?w=1400&q=80)

The sole home of the Asiatic lion in the wilderness. It is a stunning ecosystem of dry deciduous forests and savanna, teeming with leopards, antelopes, vultures, and pythons.

**Note:** The park is closed from mid-June to mid-October during the monsoon season.
