# Hawa Mahal

![Hawa Mahal](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1477587458883-47145ed94245?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&auto=format&fit=crop&w=1000&q=80)

The Palace of Winds. A magnificent pink sandstone palace with over 900 intricately carved windows built in 1799.

**Note:** The best time to take photos is early morning when the sun hits the facade.
