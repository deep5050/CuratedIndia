# India Gate

![India Gate](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1587474260584-136574528ed5?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&auto=format&fit=crop&w=1000&q=80)

A monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi, dedicated to the troops of British India who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919. A prominent landmark reflecting incredible national pride.

**Note:** Enjoy a leisurely walk along the Rajpath and taste street food nearby.
