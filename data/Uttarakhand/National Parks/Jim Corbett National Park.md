# Jim Corbett National Park

![Jim Corbett](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1589136777351-fdc9c9b58133?w=1400&q=80)

The oldest national park in India, established to protect the endangered Bengal tiger. Nestled in the Himalayan foothills, it's a paradise of hills, riverine belts, marshy depressions, and pure wilderness.

**Note:** The Dhikala zone is the most famous for tiger sightings and offers overnight stays inside the forest.
