# Kanha National Park

![Kanha](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1598929440520-dc9f1847eace?w=1400&q=80)

One of the largest national parks in India, Kanha provided the inspiration for Rudyard Kipling's classic novel "The Jungle Book". It is renowned for its significant population of Royal Bengal Tigers and Barasinghas.

**Note:** Book your Jeep Safari well in advance, especially for the Kanha and Mukki zones.
