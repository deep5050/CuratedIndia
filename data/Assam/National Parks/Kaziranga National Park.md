# Kaziranga National Park

![Kaziranga](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1662978051727-46dc8edffbc0?w=1400&q=80)

A World Heritage Site famous for hosting two-thirds of the world's great one-horned rhinoceroses. It features vast expanses of tall elephant grass, marshland, and dense tropical moist broadleaf forests.

**Note:** Best time to visit is from November to April. Take an elephant safari for the closest view of rhinos!
