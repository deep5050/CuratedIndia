# Shore Temple

![Shore Temple](https://plus.unsplash.com/premium_photo-1697729600122-eb1fe3588df8)

A gorgeous temple overlooking the shore of the Bay of Bengal.

**Note:** Particularly stunning at sunrise or sunset.

[Google Maps link](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ShoreTemple)

Built in the 8th century, it is one of the oldest structural stone temples of South India.
