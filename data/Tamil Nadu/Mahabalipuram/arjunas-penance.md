# Arjuna's Penance

![Arjuna's Penance](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1610408544837-9b2fdfde8115)

A gigantic open-air rock relief carved on two monolithic rock boulders.

**Note:** One of the largest open-air rock reliefs in the world.

[Google Maps link](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ArjunasPenance)

Depicts scenes from the Hindu epic Mahabharata.
