# Pancha Rathas

![Pancha Rathas](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1610408544837-9b2fdfde8115)

A monument complex known as the Five Chariots, carved out of a single rock.

**Note:** Not actually chariots, they are carved monoliths dedicated to the Pandavas.

[Google Maps link](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PanchaRathas)

An incredible showcase of ancient rock-cut architecture.
