Kaziranga National Park

A World Heritage Site famous for hosting two-thirds of the world's great one-horned rhinoceroses. It features vast expanses of tall elephant grass, marshland, and dense tropical moist broadleaf forests.

💡 Best time to visit is from November to April. Take an elephant safari for the closest view of rhinos!
