Kolkata

A city of joy, Kolkata is the cultural capital of India, known for its colonial architecture, vibrant street life, and rich literary heritage.

Victoria Memorial

A large marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, which was built between 1906 and 1921.

💡 It is dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria. Beautiful to visit in the evening.
